About SCET AWS Group

The SCET AWS Group was formed in 2024 under the Computer Engineering Department with the guidance of expert faculty. The group enables students to explore Amazon Web Services (AWS) cloud solutions, fostering skills in cloud computing, data management, and infrastructure development.AWS Group provides a platform for students to build and deploy scalable applications, gain hands-on experience with real-world cloud technologies, and develop solutions that address real-life challenges. Through project-based learning, students will explore AWS services, including machine learning, storage, and database management, preparing them for careers in cloud computing and beyond.

Activities Done in AWS Group

○ Workshops on AWS Cloud services, including IoT, AI/ML, and serverless computing, provide hands-on experience and explore cloud solutions for real-world applications.
○ Students have developed projects utilizing AWS technologies across various domains, such as:
○ Cloud-based Gesture Control Systems for smart car and home automation
○ Automated Classroom Management using AWS IoT
○ Serverless AC Automation with AWS Lambda
○ Drone Object Detection using AWS Rekognition
○ Automated control of classroom lights and fans through cloud integration
○ Real-time Drone Communication leveraging AWS IoT Core and Edge Computing

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions about the SCET AWS Group, aimed at helping students understand the opportunities and benefits offered.

  • Why should I join the SCET AWS Group?

    Joining the AWS Group allows you to develop cloud computing skills, gain hands-on experience with AWS services, and build innovative solutions for real-world challenges.

  • What types of projects are undertaken in the AWS Group?

    Students work on projects involving cloud-based applications, IoT solutions, AI/ML models, and robotics, aiming to solve practical problems and gain familiarity with emerging technologies.

  • What activities are organized in the AWS Group?

    We conduct workshops, hackathons, and expert talks on AWS services and other advanced technologies, offering students practical experience with cloud computing and hardware integration.

  • Will there be workshops and expert sessions?

    Yes, the AWS Group regularly arranges workshops and expert sessions to help students adopt new technologies, build projects, and resolve any technical queries.

  • How can I ask questions or raise concerns?

    You can submit your questions or concerns through the contact form on our website, and we’ll get back to you with the necessary guidance.

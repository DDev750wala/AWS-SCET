About SCET AWS Group

The SCET AWS Group was established in 2024 under the Computer Engineering Department, with guidance from experienced faculty members. This group supports students in building innovative solutions and developing technical skills, particularly in cloud computing, IoT, and AI. Since its inception, the AWS Group has aimed to equip students with the skills needed to tackle real-world problems by working on projects that blend software and hardware technologies.

Vision

The AWS Group at the Computer Engineering Department provides a platform for students with the following visions:
○ To enhance leadership skills
○ To explore interdisciplinary knowledge
○ To provide exposure to new technologies
○ To showcase technical skills and share knowledge among peers
○ To build a stronger community that fosters teamwork, enhancing coding and hardware skills

Activities in AWS Group

○ Workshops on IoT, practical experience with hardware, and advanced technology topics such as robotics, drones, and computer vision.
○ Students develop projects across various domains, including:
- Gesture-based car control
- Home automation using gestures
- Automated classroom projector systems
- AC automation
- Object or human tracking drones
- Classroom light/fan automation
- Drone communication systems
